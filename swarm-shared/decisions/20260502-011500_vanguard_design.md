* The absence of a README file hinders the onboarding process for new team members and makes it challenging for them to contribute to the project.
* The project's design and architecture are not well-documented, leading to potential misunderstandings and misimplementations.
* The lack of a clear project overview and guidelines makes it hard for developers to navigate the codebase and identify areas for improvement.
