- **Lack of Documentation**: The absence of a README file and design documentation hinders understanding of the project's purpose, setup, usage, and design principles.

- **No Contribution Guidelines**: Without guidelines, potential contributors may be unsure how to engage with the project effectively.
- **Missing License Information**: The absence of a license file can lead to legal ambiguities regarding the use and distribution of the project.
